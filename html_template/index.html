Background & Interests

I am from a small rural town in New Hampshire called Loudon. Outside of scientific research I enjoy both playing & watching sports, specifically basketball, which I have played since I was 6 years old and follow my favorite team, the Boston Celtics. Growing up I developed an affinity for the outdoors, spending lots of time hiking & camping with my Dad and friends. Additionally I enjoyed travelling around the New England region with my family, which in combination with an outdoorsman attitude gave me a strong sense of exploration.

I have continued to pursue this sense of adventure in my life, moving across the country at 18 to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Oregon where I encountered a welcoming and friendly atmosphere accompanying some breath-takingly views of nature (along with copious amounts of rain). After finishing my studies there and a brief stint of working back home, I decided to further my intellectual pursuits and move to Italy. This experience has been enriching across so many levels from professional to personal, every day I enjoy the multitude of conversations that come with interacting with other international students from all over the world. I highly value the past experiences that I have sought out and I am eager & optimistic about what life still has in store for me.
